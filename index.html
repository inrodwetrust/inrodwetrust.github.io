I am a physician with an interest in medical app development and data science. I am learning full-stack web development in order to streamline much of the diagnostic and treatment protocols in medicine. Most diagnosis utilizes a series of algorithms; if A, B and C are true, then you have a specific diagnosis. The majority of this process can be automated in Python or the like.

The benefit of this is that the patient gets a diagnosis correct statistically most of the time. Clinicians are human and they can be tired, irritated, unwell, or just not having a good day. Mistakes happen when human are any of these things. Computers never tire and we can better isolate specific variables of performance, accordingly.

I have an entrepreneurial spirit, so I have further interest in creating and marketing medical applications that patients and doctors can use to better manage patient cases. I strongly believe that technology, if in the right hands, could be used to improve healthcare, reduce the cost to the patient, and increase job satisfaction for clinicans.
